Prototype development

We create a prototype based on your ideas and needs. Starting with a simple wiregrid prototype as to see how the layout will be. We work closely with our customers to design the website, that way we are able to capture the wanted design of each customer.

Design

Based on the prototype we can design the website. Every aspect of it is well thought of and the customer's ideas are taken into consideration regarding color choice and more.

Programming

Using the design agreed upon, the programming of the website can start. By working closely with the customer every step of the way ensures that the customer is always kept in the loop and we give regular status updates of the development of the website.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

We help you with getting your website out on the web. With SEO tools we make sure that search engines such as Google, or Bing can find your website and deliver them to the customers.

Analytics

We provide you with analytical statistis tool offered by Google. Using the analytical tool you can see how many page visits you are getting and from where in the world.

Hosting

We can handle all the hosting of our customers. We use a third-party hosting service based upon the needs of the customers and the functions of the website. If you already have a hosting service you just need to provide us with access to that and we will take care of the rest.

Support

After the development and initial deployment of the website we provide support service to our customers. If there is a problem with the website of any sort we discuss how to take care of it and fix it as soon as possible.
